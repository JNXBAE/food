How we started

A love letter to slow-cooked flavour

Jungle Foods opened on the Cuddalore–Chidambaram Highway with one goal: bring Arabian Mandi, home-style Biriyani and wok-fresh Chinese together under a single, unmistakably fun roof. What began as a small highway stop serving Chicken Mandi has grown into a full multi-cuisine kitchen — without losing the smoky, slow-cooked soul it started with.

Every dish on our menu, from a ₹6 Coin Parotta to a full Bucket Biriyani, is made fresh in-house, the same day it's served.
